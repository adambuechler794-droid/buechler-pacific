Month-End Close Transformation

10-day close · cash flow: 3 people → 1 person

Transformed the entire month-end close through data pipeline automation, standardized reconciliation workflows, and AI-assisted review. The cash flow statement went from three senior staff working a full day to one person under an hour.

Talk About Close Automation See the underlying platform

The Challenge

The monthly close was consuming too many calendar days, delaying leadership’s access to reliable numbers. The cash flow statement alone required the CFO, Controller, and FP&A Director to work together over a full day every month.

The Solution

Streamlined the close process end-to-end with automated data pipelines, standardized reconciliation workflows, and AI-assisted review at each stage. For the cash flow statement, built a Power Pivot model pulling directly from the Fabric semantic layer with a monthly human review step.

Results

  • 10 business day close cycle achieved
  • Cash flow statement: three people, full day → one person, under an hour
  • 90% automated with human verification step
  • Freed senior finance staff to focus on analysis during close
