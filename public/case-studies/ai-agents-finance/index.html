AI Agents for Financial Planning & Data Analysis

Data retrieval → strategic analysis

Two purpose-built AI agents: one that interfaces directly with NetSuite for planning, budgeting, and financial Q&A, and another that connects to Microsoft Fabric for ad hoc data analysis and exploration. Both allow natural-language interaction with live financial systems.

Talk About Your Finance Stack See the Platform Demo

The Challenge

Finance teams spend significant time on repetitive queries: pulling budget versus actual comparisons, checking forecast assumptions, and exploring data across dimensions. These tasks consume analyst time even when the questions are straightforward.

The Solution

Built two purpose-built AI agents, each connected to a live business system. One focuses on planning and budgeting through NetSuite. The other handles exploratory analysis through Microsoft Fabric. Together they turn natural language into immediate, system-grounded answers.

Results

  • Finance professionals query ERP and data platform systems in plain English
  • Immediate answers without writing queries or pulling exports
  • Analyst bandwidth shifted from data retrieval to strategic analysis
  • Two agents covering both planning and analysis workflows
